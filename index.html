✦ Our Story

Born from a Passion for Pure, Honest Food

Stellar Restaurant began as a dream — to prove that vegetarian cuisine can be just as exciting, indulgent, and satisfying as anything else on the planet.

✦ Who We Are

Ahmedabad's Finest Vegetarian Dining

Founded in 2012, Stellar Restaurant set out to reimagine vegetarian cuisine in Ahmedabad. What started as a small 30-cover restaurant on SG Highway has grown into one of the city's most celebrated culinary destinations — without ever compromising on our founding promise: 100% vegetarian, always fresh, always extraordinary.

We draw from the rich tapestry of Indian culinary traditions — from the spice routes of Rajasthan to the coastal kitchens of South India — while embracing the finest techniques from Italian, Mediterranean, and Pan-Asian cooking. The result is a menu that is boldly vegetarian and globally inspired.

Every dish served at Stellar is a reflection of our belief that the best meals are made with care, curiosity, and the very best seasonal ingredients.
